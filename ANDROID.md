## System Requirements

- **Android Version**: Android 5.0 (Lollipop) or higher
- **Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, or Samsung Internet (latest version recommended)
- **Storage**: ~5 MB of space
- **Internet**: Required for initial installation and updates (offline mode available after installation)

## Troubleshooting

### "Add to Home screen" option not showing?
- Make sure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Firefox, Samsung Internet)
- Ensure you're visiting the site via HTTPS (secure connection)
- Try refreshing the page

### App not working offline?
- Open the app at least once while online to cache the content
- Clear browser cache and reinstall if issues persist

### Voice features not working?
- Make sure your device volume is turned up
- Grant microphone/audio permissions if prompted
- Some older Android devices may have limited speech synthesis support
